CHAAT
  • Time:6 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
papdi 13
boiled and chopped potato 3 tbsp
boiled chickpea 3 tbsp
finely chopped onion 3 tbsp
whisked curd 5 tbsp
green chutney 3 tbsp
tamarind chutney 3 tbsp
chilli powder pinch
cumin powder pinch
chaat masala pinch
a salt pinch
finely copped tomato 1 tbsp
sev 3 tbsp
finely chopped coriander 1 tbsp

Step 1:firstly, in a serving plate take 13 papdi. to prepare homemade papdi using wheat flour check out my papdi recipe.
Step 2:top with 3 tbsp potato and 3 tbsp chickpea..
Step 3:also, sprinkle 2 tbsp of chopped onions..
Step 4:now take 3 tbsp of whisked curd and drizzle over it.
Step 5:further, add 2 tsp of green chutney and 2 tsp of tamarind chutney.
Step 6:sprinkle chilli powder, cumin powder, chaat masala and salt.
Step 7:additionally, add 1 tbsp onion and 1 tbsp tomato.
Step 8:now add 1 tbsp of more curd, 1 tsp green chutney and 1 tsp tamarind chutney.
Step 9:top with 3 tbsp sev and garnish with 1 tsp coriander.
Step 10:finally, enjoy dahi papdi chaat with a hot cup of tea..
